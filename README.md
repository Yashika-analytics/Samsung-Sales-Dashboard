# 📱 Samsung Global Sales Dashboard - Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 15,500+ Samsung product 
sales transactions across 52 countries from 2021 to 2024.

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
1. Which regions and countries generate the most revenue?
2. How has Samsung's revenue trended from 2021-2024?
3. Which products are the top revenue generators?
4. What percentage of revenue comes from 5G devices?
5. Which months have the highest sales performance?

## 💡 Key Insights
- 🌍 **Asia and North America** are the top performing regions globally
- 📈 **Q4** consistently shows the strongest quarterly performance
- 📱 **62.79% of revenue** comes from 5G devices — showing clear market shift
- 🏆 **Samsung Neo Q series** leads all products in total revenue
- 📅 **July** is surprisingly the strongest month across all years

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop**
- **DAX** for calculated measures
- **Dataset:** Samsung Global Product Sales from Kaggle

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- ✅ Interactive year filter (2021-2024)
- ✅ KPI cards — Total Revenue, Units Sold, Countries, Avg Rating
- ✅ Global revenue map with drill down
- ✅ Revenue trends line chart
- ✅ 5G vs Non-5G donut chart
- ✅ Top products bar chart
- ✅ Monthly revenue trends
